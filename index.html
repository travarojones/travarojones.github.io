About Me

I remember my younger days. Days I spent reading comics, trying to recreate my favorite heroes with a freshly sharpened pencil and a piece of notebook paper. Each story soon became a creation of my own design. I'll never forget opening that beautiful Sega Genesis on January 23rd, 1999 (my 7th birthday). I had 3 games: Sonic the Hedgehog, NBA Jam and Micheal Jackson's Moonwalker. I played those 3 games for countless hours in a room that was always "supposed to be clean 20 minutes ago" (you can guess who said that). What's the tech behind this, how do I create one: questions that scintillated a life long journey that has been at times challenging, but always worth it.

Fast forward to 2018. I've spent most of my adult life working jobs I hated, telling myself there has to be something better out there, looking in the mirror for that little kid who believed that anything was possible. That same litle kid who would be overly optimistic, attempting to alleviate any doubt I had, or alternatively suggest I simply learn something new. That was the answer I was looking for. I'm inquisitive by nature. A self-proclaimed master problem solver. I was never going to be happy working jobs that didn't force me to continue learning. After reading an online job posting for a software developer, I knew exactly what I had to do. Thus began, My Pursuit Of Happiness.

— TCJ, Modern Lifer
