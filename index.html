About Me

I remember my younger days. Days I spent reading comics, trying to recreate my favorite heroes with a freshly sharpened pencil and a piece of notebook paper. Each story soon became a creation of my own design. I remember singing along to my favorite songs as I glared out of the passenger side window of my mom's Ford Bronco II. I often replaced lines or verses with some of my own, treating instrumentals like Mad Libs, the sillier the better. I'll never forget opening that beautiful Sega Genesis on January 23rd, 1999 (my 7th birthday). I had 3 games: Sonic the Hedgehog, NBA Jam and Micheal Jackson's Moonwalker. I played those 3 games for countless hours in a room that was always "supposed to be clean 20 minutes ago" (you can guess who said that). Dumbfounded at video games and how they worked, I wanted to create one, but how? That question scintillated a life long journey that has been at times challenging, but always worth it.
